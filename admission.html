LKG Admission Criteria

Age Requirement:
  • Children seeking admission to LKG must have completed 3 years of age.
  • The child must be born on or before 31st July 2022.
Mandatory Documents:
  • Birth Certificate (Photocopy): The child’s name must be registered in the certificate.
  • Aadhaar Card (Photocopy)
  • Caste Proof Document (TC or Caste Certificate)
  • Smart Card (Ration Card) showing parent and child’s names

Admission to other Classes (UKG to Class X)

Mandatory Documents:
  • Birth Certificate (Photocopy)
  • Caste Certificate (Photocopy)
  • Smart Card (Family Ration Card) – Photocopy
  • Transfer Certificate (TC) – From the previously attended school.

Admission to Class XI (+1)

Available Streams for Higher Secondary:
Science Group Options:
  • Group I: Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, Biology
  • Group II: Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, Computer Science
  • Group III: Physics, Chemistry, Botany, Zoology
Commerce & Humanities Group Options:
  • Group IV: Commerce, Accountancy, Economics, Computer Application
  • Group V: Geography, History, Economics, Computer Application
General Information:
  • Admissions are open for all grades from LKG to Class XII.
  • All documents must be submitted as photocopies during the application process. Originals may be required for verification.
  • Admission is subject to availability of seats and fulfilment of eligibility criteria.

About Us

In 1987, inspired by the visit of former President of India Dr. Zakir Husain to the village mosque inauguration years earlier, the Forum, with the generous support of Alhaj E.S.M. Bakker Mohamed, established the Dr. Zakir Husain English School. It began humbly with just 26 students in LKG and UKG.
